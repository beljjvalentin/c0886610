How to make classic iced tea:

  1. firstly, in a blender take 2 tbsp mint, 3 tsp lemon juice and ½ cup water.
  2. blend to smooth mint juice. keep aside.
  3. in a tall glass take few ice cubes, 2 slices of lemon and few mint.
  4. pour in prepared mint juice and chilled tea decoction.
  5. finally, mix well and enjoy classic iced tea.

How to make mango iced tea:

  2. blend to smooth mango juice. keep aside.
  3. in a tall glass take few ice cubes and few chopped mango.

  5. finally, mix well and enjoy mango iced tea.
